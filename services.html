What We Do

Services & Capabilities

End-to-end filament manufacturing from concept to delivery, with supporting extrusion processes and operations.

Custom Filament Extrusion

Precision extrusion of 3D printer filaments using customer-provided compounds and engineered formulation blends. We manufacture multi-property blend polymers with tight diameter tolerances and in-line laser micrometer monitoring.

R&D / Testing / Sampling

New product development from concept to production. Send us your polymers for viability testing on our extrusion production machines to produce a variety of filaments for evaluation.

Private Labeling

White-label manufacturing with your branding and packaging specifications. Private labeling for use with 3D printers, with custom packaging and labeling to your exact requirements.

Special Diameters & Colors

Industry standard and custom diameters available. Industry standard colors with custom color matching. Non-standard filament sizes engineered to your specifications.

Bulk Packaging & Drop Shipping

Standard 2.2 lb / 1 kg spools, bulk packaging, many spool types including returnable plastic and cardboard disposable. Vacuum sealed with desiccant insert. Drop shipping available.

Quality Inspection & Monitoring

Filament diameter verified by 3-stage inspection process. In-line laser micrometer monitoring during production. Pre-winding and pre-shipping inspection on every batch.

Custom Compound Extrusion

We specialize in extruding customer-provided compounds and engineered formulation blends into precision 3D printer filaments. Our production capabilities support a wide range of polymer types and specialty additives, allowing us to manufacture filaments that meet your exact performance requirements.

  • Customer-provided compounds and engineered blends
  • Multi-property blend polymer manufacturing
  • Special additive extrusion
  • Smooth-Sol ABS
  • Printer Purging Filament
  • Micro Filament
  • Grinding and reprocessing services

Quality Assurance & Packaging

Every filament batch goes through our rigorous 3-stage inspection process. From in-line laser micrometer monitoring during extrusion to pre-winding and pre-shipping checks, we ensure consistent diameter tolerances and reliable quality on every spool we produce.

  • Filament diameter verified by 3-stage inspection process
  • In-line laser micrometer monitoring
  • Pre-winding / pre-shipping inspection
  • Standard spool sizes 2.2 lb / 1 kg
  • Plastic spools returnable for refill
  • Cardboard throw-away spools available
  • Vacuum sealed with desiccant insert
  • Custom packaging available
  • Custom color matching available
